Let’s Play the Advanced Quiz Game!

How about seeing for how long you can keep the lead? Step up and take on this breath-taking quiz where you’ve got it all to play for! It just so happens that you will go through a number of interesting and thought provoking questions which are meant to be aimed at evaluating your general knowledge, your memory and even the way you approach and solve problems. The following is what will happen1:

1) You have 100 seconds to answer all the questions – don’t let the time slip away!
2) Choose the correct option out of the options available to you. Make good choices for every selection you make because it will move you closer to wining.
3) When a time limit is reached, the game will terminate itself, but there is always an opportunity to improve your best score through the games.
4) Do you want to challenge your brain and do it against the clock? Click "Start" to start the game and good luck!
